The Insta360 X4 360° 8K Camera is here with the best ever image quality, offering creators unbelievable clarity with every streak of colour in unprecedent detail and unshakable FlowState Stabilization performance. With advanced technology, the Insta360 X4 allows you to experience smoother, sharp images for every frame with an improved 60fps, making it ideal for action sports or scenes with lots of motion.

Key features include 360° Active HDR video mode that brings out the details and maintains superb stabilization for action sports, 67% longer run time than the X3, that gets you 135 minutes of recording time at 5.7K at 30 fps, 72MP stunning images with more clarity and detail, beautiful video footage with Single-Lens mode in 4K at 60fps or take it further and go ultra-wide with MaxView in FreeFrame Video, featuring a 170° view in an upgraded 4K at 30fps.
